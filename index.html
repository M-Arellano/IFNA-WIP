IFNA Mission

The International Federation of Nonlinear Analysts (IFNA) is a not-for-profit educational and research oriented organization (society), that was founded more than 25 years ago, with the ambitious goal of assisting in changing our world one topic at a time. IFNA is globally an interdisciplinary professional organization which seeks to promote the understanding of related complex nonlinear problems and approaches to solutions from all disciplines using the art of mathematical sciences.

This focus on common themes has led to IFNA’s motto of understanding through
“GLOBAL DIVERSITY, COOPERATION AND COLLABORATION”.
IFNA researchers from all fields of study from across the GLOBE seek solutions to real world problems facing our society in this age of information. There has been a growing need for this professional organization to assist in increased understanding of our Global Society. With more than 125 countries including developed, developing and undeveloped countries, IFNA serves as a global network where instant communication allows for assistance to be made Anywhere, Anytime, Anyplace.

Our globalization and technological evolution is becoming more complex, highly interactive and interdependent. Furthermore, the 21st century advances in technology have increasingly generated and used large, complex, and diverse datasets (BIG DATA). Such advancements in technology have engendered new problems that are different in nature and more global in scope. For example, now there are many serious problems of worldwide consequences, in GLOBAL WARMING, GLOBAL ECONOMY, GLOBAL HEALTH, GLOBAL ENERGY, among others. The better understanding of such complex problems requires the joint efforts of researchers from all disciplines including chemical, biological, ecological, economic, engineering, environmental, medical, physical, and sociological sciences, among others. With Mathematical and Statistical Sciences as a conceptual language and tool, we can address all global problems in all aspects of theory, methods and applications.

We at “IFNA WORLD” focus on “The Circles of Sustainability” as so clearly defined by the United Nations.

IFNA World Goals and Objectives:

Create opportunities for the exchange of ideas among the scholars and bridge the cultural boundaries.
Help researchers from all fields of study across the globe to resolve current real world problems.
Encourage undergraduate and graduate exchange programs.
Encourage and assist educational activities in developed and undeveloped countries.
Assist in the creation of database that is essential for research and decision making.
Provide financial aid assistance international activities in education and research.
Use 21st century technology to develop partnership among researchers, scientists, and agencies around the globe that work on complex problems.
Build problem solving groups among the scholars around the globe.
Promote global centers for their innovative and cooperative solutions to the global real problems.
Host international conferences and other academic activities to enhance the education in the developing countries.
Establish communication between global centers using social media, web-based content, and traditional media.
Enhance the quality of life through research and development.
